My Projects

Here are some of the projects that were done either in school or during my free time.

Food Court Menu System

Over the course of a month, me and my team developed a prototype of a simple application that allows students to view stall information and queue time of NTU's food court. The functional requirements of the application include allowing students to view today's stalls, view stalls by date and estimate queue time. On selected days of the week, the menu of stalls will also include a special menu.

Tech Stack: Python (Tkinter, PIL, PPrint)

My Bakery App

A real-time mobile application quickly developed and scaled using Flutter which supports both Android and IOS environment.

Users are required to first create an account before they can log in. When logged in, users will be able to make new orders, view past orders, view amount spent and change passwords. Data collected from the application is stored in JSON format using Google's Firebase Cloud Store. This allows for large volumes of data to be handled at high speed and easy updates to schemas.

Tech Stack: Dart, Ruby, NoSQL (Firebase Cloud)

Analytics Model to Improve Onboarding Processes

My team and I developed a Classification and Regression Tree (CART) Model as well as a Logistic Regression model to predict and evaluate the likelihood of clients to onboard wth the company. High valued customers should be the target for company's marketing campaigns and there is a business need to accurately predict the effectiveness of a marketing campaign to onboard the targeted audience. Our group processed historical data and built predictive models to identify high-valued customers.

Tech Stack: R (CART, ggplot)
